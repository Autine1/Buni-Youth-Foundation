About Buni Youth Foundation

Buni Youth Foundation is a community that was founded in the year '2012'
most importantly, this group was ment to establish, address and solve some of the
issue and challenges youth face in the society, some of the issues are:


  • Gender violence
  • Drug abuse and addiction
  • Youth stress
  • Sexual harassment
  • Early marriages

    • most importantly, the group spread awareness to the youths
    to say no to all of them.
    Incase of anything happen to any youth
    anywhere no matter the gender,age,or
    region, if reported to us; we will stand in the gap to help. We are located at Nairobi, Moi avenue opposite Bahai towers.
